# Superstore Sales Dashboard

## Project Overview

The Superstore Sales Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional trends. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and key performance indicators (KPIs).

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing categories and sub-categories.
* Compare sales and profit across different regions.
* Track monthly sales trends and business growth.
* Understand customer segment performance.
* Support data-driven business decision-making.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Microsoft Excel

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Profit Margin

### Regional Analysis

* Sales by Region

### Customer Segment Analysis

* Consumer Segment Performance
* Corporate Segment Performance
* Home Office Segment Performance

## Key Insights

* Technology category generated the highest sales revenue.
* Certain sub-categories showed strong sales but lower profitability.
* Regional performance varied significantly across locations.
* Monthly sales trends revealed seasonal business patterns.
* Customer segments contributed differently to overall revenue and profit.
